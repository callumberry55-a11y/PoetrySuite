## Distribution Strategy

### Equal Monthly Allocation
- **Rationale:** Ensures predictable and stable funding throughout the year
- **Flexibility:** Allows for consistent community growth without seasonal variance
- **Transparency:** Simple, clear allocation model easy to understand

### Usage Scenarios

1. **Weekly User Bonuses**
   - 10 points per user per week
   - Funded from monthly allocation
   - Encourages consistent engagement

2. **Developer Rewards**
   - 50% of tax revenue
   - Distributed from developer payment pool
   - Incentivizes PaaS development

3. **Badge Achievements**
   - Variable rewards (50-500 points)
   - Drawn from monthly allocation
   - Recognizes user accomplishments

4. **Contest Prizes**
   - Special allocations for competitions
   - Funded from designated pools
   - Promotes community participation
